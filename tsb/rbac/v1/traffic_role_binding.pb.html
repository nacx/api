TrafficRoleBinding is an assignment of roles to a set of users or teams to access resources under a Traffic group. The user or team information is obtained from an LDAP server that should have been configured as part of Service Bridge installation. Note that a TrafficRoleBinding can be created or modified only by users who have set_rbac permission on the Traffic group.

The following example assigns the traffic-admin role to users alice, bob, and members of the traffic-ops team for traffic group g1 under workspace w1 owned by the tenant mycompany.

apiVersion: rbac.tsb.tetrate.io/v1
kind: TrafficRoleBinding
metadata:
  name: traffic-g1
  tenant: mycompany
  workspace: w1
  group: g1
spec:
  role: traffic-admin
  subjects:
  - user: alice
  - user: bob
  - team: traffic-ops
