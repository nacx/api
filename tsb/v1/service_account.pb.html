ServiceAccount represents a local service account that can be used to log. into the platform. Service accounts are usually issued by the paltform itself for the different agents, but can be used to integrate with the platform. The following example creates a service account named check under the tenant mycompany .

apiVersion: api.tsb.tetrate.io/v1
kind: ServiceAccount
metadata:
  name: check
  tenant: mycompany
spec:
  principalName: check
